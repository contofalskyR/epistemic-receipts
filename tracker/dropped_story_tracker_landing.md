## WHAT IT DOES

Every issue you track becomes a **thread** — one clear question with a live status:

- **🟢 Developing** — moving right now.
- **🔴 Stalled** — no movement, but waiting on a known next step (a ruling, a vote, a deadline).
- **⚪ Resolved** — settled, with any successor fight flagged automatically.
- **🟠 Orphaned** — still unresolved, but everyone stopped covering it. **This is the alert nobody else sends.**

You get a **board, not a feed**: every fight, its status, its last real movement, and a one-line "here's where it stands." Plus an alert the moment a dormant thread reactivates — or a still-live one falls off everyone's radar.
