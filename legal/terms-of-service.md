## 4. What the site does

Epistemic Receipts provides:

- A searchable database of factual claims with sources, provenance metadata, and epistemic-status labels.
- A public API for programmatic access to claims, sources, and graph structure.
- Topic-based email alerts: you can subscribe to a topic and receive weekly email notifications when new claims are added.
- A bookmarking feature that lets you save claims using a client-generated key. No account or password is required for bookmarks.
- A feedback form for submitting corrections, suggestions, or comments.

The site does **not** provide: legal advice, medical advice, financial advice, or any other professional advisory service. Do not rely on claims from this database as a substitute for professional advice.
